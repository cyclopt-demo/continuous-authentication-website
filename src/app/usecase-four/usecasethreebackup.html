Third Use Case Scenario: Writing paragraphs

The present and last use case scenario is also very simple: Pure writing of a given text.
We present you this case scenario in order to get as much as keystroke data we can from your patterns and timings in order to create a keystroke profile that matches as much as possible your real one.

You are given three paragraphs, containing random sentences from random texts and the only thing you have to do is type the text you see to the textarea below.
Of course you don't have to type all the text you see, but the more text you type, the higher the probability of the system of kicking a hacker out of your profile. ☺
